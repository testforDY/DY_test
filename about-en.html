About Dyfenco

Dyfenco Co., Ltd., founded in 1979, was established by a team of experts with extensive experience in materials science and packaging processes. We specialize in the R&D and manufacturing of electronic soldering materials, providing high-quality and reliable raw materials to the global electronics industry.

Our product range includes solder paste, solder wire, solder bar, flux, tin anodes, preform solder, and electronic packaging adhesives, widely applied in advanced manufacturing such as PCB assembly, automotive electronics, LED, semiconductor packaging, PCs, and communication devices.

With operations in Taiwan, China, Vietnam, and India, we are continuously expanding our global footprint and enhancing localized support. In 2012, we established the MORE Electronics Laboratory, which follows IPC international standards to offer full-scale product testing, application validation, and process simulation.

We firmly believe that consistent quality, continuous innovation, and deep technical expertise are the keys to becoming a trusted long-term partner.
